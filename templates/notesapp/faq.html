Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Whitepaper

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password. Once registered, you can start creating and organizing your notes immediately.

Is Whitepaper free to use?

Yes, Whitepaper offers a free tier with basic features. You can create unlimited notes, use tags for organization, and access your notes from any device. Premium features may be available for advanced users.

Using the Application

How do I create a new note?

To create a new note, click the "New Note" button or use the editor area. You can then type your content, add formatting, and save the note. Your notes are automatically saved as you type.

How do I organize my notes with tags?

When creating or editing a note, you can add tags in the tag input field. Separate multiple tags with commas. You can then filter and search your notes by these tags to quickly find what you need.

Can I share my notes with others?

Yes, you can share your notes with other users. Use the share feature to generate a link or invite specific users to view your notes. You can control the permissions for shared notes.

How do I search through my notes?

Use the search bar to find notes by title, content, or tags. The search is real-time and will show results as you type. You can also use advanced search filters to narrow down your results.

Data & Security

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and regularly audit our systems to ensure your information is protected.

Can I export my notes?

Yes, you can export your notes in various formats including plain text, markdown, and PDF. This feature is available in your account settings.

What happens if I delete my account?

When you delete your account, all your notes and data will be permanently removed from our servers. We recommend exporting your data before deleting your account if you want to keep a backup.

Technical Support

What browsers are supported?

Whitepaper works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience, we recommend using the latest version of your browser.

Can I use Whitepaper on mobile devices?

Yes, Whitepaper is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. You can access all features through your mobile browser, and the interface adapts to your screen size.

How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password securely.
